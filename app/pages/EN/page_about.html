DrewMet Anton Paulau is an international, dynamically developing company, the priority of which is the production and sale of sawn timber/wood in Poland, Lithuania, Latvia, USA, Europe . Sawing of round timber/wood is carried out by highly qualified specialists using modern equipment

We offer high quality lumber:

  • Longitudinally

  • Edged

  • Unedged

The production of lumber is carried out in the quantity, nomenclature and the dimensions of the customer. The manufactured products correspond to the State standard and have a certificate of their own production

A wide selection of various timber/wood species:

  • Spruce

  • Pine

  • Birch

  • Aspen

  • Alder

Our company offers a wide selection of lumber at profitable prices, and invites you to mutually beneficial cooperation


We guarantee:

  • Prompt delivery

  • Products of the high quality

  • Individual approach to each client
