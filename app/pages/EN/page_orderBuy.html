Depending on your needs, DrewMet Anton Paulau company is ready to organize the supply of products of the required dimensions according to the specification. We offer both natural moisture (wet) and machine-dried wood in drying chambers up to 20% moisture (KD).

If necessary, treatment against fungus (blue stain infection), high-quality wood preservation treatment can be performed.

To determine the price, we need the dimensions of the product, the species and type of wood, the requirements for labeling and packaging, the degree of moisture (up to 10%, 11-15%, 16-20%).

DrewMet Anton Paulau aims at strong long-term cooperation and is ready quickly and efficiently to organize wholesale distribution of products to the United States and any country of the European Union.

We are waiting for your specifications (applications) and our prices will delight you.
